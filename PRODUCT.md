# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Modern Indonesian women and men equally, aged 25–40. Shopping on mobile and desktop for fashion that honours Indonesia's textile heritage without feeling folksy or touristy. Context: browsing leisurely, discovering new pieces, comparing styles. Secondary user: the store owner operating the admin dashboard to manage products, orders, and customers.

## Product Purpose

Taly is an Indonesian fashion brand and e-commerce platform that sells clothing and accessories made with local artisan craftsmanship (batik, weaving, linen). It celebrates Indonesia's rich textile traditions through modern, minimal silhouettes. Currently a mockup; no live functionality required. Success looks like: a customer feels the brand is elevated yet accessible, and the store owner can operate their business efficiently.

## Brand Personality

Elegant, modern, Indonesian. Warm without being nostalgic. Culturally rooted without being folkloric. The voice is quiet and confident, not promotional.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS/tech aesthetics: blue accent buttons, identical card grids, hero metric dashboards, "Proceed to Checkout" in a blue pill button.
- Fast fashion visual language: cluttered grids, sale banners, aggressive CTAs.
- Cold luxury: untouchable marble, pure black, brands that feel intimidating rather than inviting.
- Batik-cliché tropicana: touristy or overly folksy presentation of the Indonesian identity.

## Design Principles

1. **Craft over commerce.** Every interaction should feel considered, not transactional. The brand story is always visible even inside functional flows.
2. **Warm restraint.** The palette is warm, never cold. Minimal, never bare. There is always texture in the neutrals.
3. **Indonesian without explanation.** Cultural identity comes through material references, silhouette names, and craft language, not icons or patterns.
4. **Confidence through quietness.** No shouting. No urgency patterns. A confident brand does not beg.
5. **Dashboard is a back-room, not a stage.** The admin interface is efficient and calm, using the same palette but optimised for scan-and-act, not impression-making.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA as a baseline. Mobile-first given Indonesian smartphone usage patterns. Colour contrast must pass for both the warm background (#F5F2EE) and white card surfaces.
